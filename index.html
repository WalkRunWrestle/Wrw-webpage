Quick questions

Straight answers. No nonsense.

Can I walk it?

Yes. Walking is welcome. You can walk, run, or mix the two.

Do I need to be fit?

No. Pick the challenge that feels doable for where you are now.

Can I do it all in one go?

Only if that challenge allows it. Some WRW challenges have daily rules.

What counts as proof?

A screenshot from your fitness app, health tracker, step count, running app or similar.

When is my medal sent?

After completion and proof checks. WRW dispatches medals on Fridays where possible.

Do you track my location?

No. WRW does not track GPS or live location. You track your own activity and send proof.

The small print View
What WRW provides

WRW gives you the challenge, app access, digital extras where included, and the physical medal where included.

Who can take part

You need to be 18+, or have permission from a parent or guardian.

Refunds

Digital access cannot be refunded once used. If you cannot complete the challenge, physical medals have a 30-day money-back guarantee before dispatch. Once a medal has been sent, it cannot be refunded.

Privacy

WRW does not sell your data and does not track GPS or live location.

Community rules

Be decent. Support people. Do not abuse, harass, bully, cheat or spam. If someone ruins the space, they can be removed.

Fitness disclaimer

WRW challenges are for fun, fitness and motivation. You take part at your own risk. If you are unsure about your health, check with a doctor first.
