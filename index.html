Help

Plain answers. No waffle.

FAQ, proof, delivery, charity and the small print.

Can I walk it?

Yes. Walking is welcome. You can walk, run, or mix the two.

Do I need to do it all at once?

No. Follow the rules of your chosen challenge. Some are daily, some are flexible, and some are streak based.

How do I track progress?

Use the WRW Hub to log your progress and keep your challenge together.

How does proof work?

Use the Hub when you finish. Need help? Get in touch and we will help you sort it.

When are medals sent?

Medals are sent after your challenge is completed and proof has been checked.

Do you ship outside the UK?

Yes. UK delivery is included and international delivery is available.

Small print

Useful bits.

Refunds

Digital access cannot be refunded once used. Physical medals can be refunded before dispatch if you cannot complete the challenge.

Privacy

WRW does not sell your data and does not track GPS or live location.

Community rules

Be decent. Support people. Do not abuse, harass, bully, cheat or spam.

Fitness disclaimer

Take part at your own risk. If you are unsure about your health, check with a doctor first.

Charity

A portion of WRW entries supports mental health charities.

Need help?

Use the contact option from the shop or community if you get stuck.
