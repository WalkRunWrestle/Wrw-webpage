Quick questions

Plain answers. No waffle.

Can I walk it?

Yes. Walking is welcome. You can walk, run, or mix the two.

Do I need to be fit?

No. Pick the challenge that feels doable for where you are now.

Can I do it all in one go?

Only if that challenge allows it. Some WRW challenges have daily rules.

What counts as proof?

A screenshot from your fitness app, health tracker, step count, running app or similar.

When is my medal sent?

After completion and proof checks. WRW dispatches medals on Fridays where possible.

Do you track my location?

No. WRW does not track GPS or live location. You track your own activity and send proof.

Terms, refunds, privacy & safety View
What WRW provides

Virtual challenge experiences, app access, digital rewards, certificates, community spaces and physical medals where included.

Who can take part

You must be 18+ or have permission from a parent or guardian.

Refunds

Digital products are non-refundable once accessed. Physical medals have a 30-day money-back guarantee if you cannot complete the challenge. No refunds are made once a medal has been dispatched.

Privacy

WRW does not sell your data and does not track GPS or live location.

Community rules

Respect people, encourage others and celebrate progress. Abuse, harassment, bullying, cheating or spamming may result in removal from WRW spaces.

Fitness disclaimer

WRW challenges are for fun, fitness and motivation. By taking part, you confirm you are medically fit and understand walking and running carry risk. Check with a doctor if unsure.
